This site allows you to create an encrypted message.

To do this you have to put values in the dedicated spaces,

marked with a word above: the hour rounded down, the day

of the week expressed by a number, the date or the day

of the month and the year.

if you need the variables at the exact moment you can

hit the button on the right that says "Auto const".

after having entered the variables in the two different 

ways you can enter the keyword for the encryption of the 

message, consisting only of letters. then write the message, 

always in the appropriate box, and press the "Encrypt" or 

"Decrypt" buttons with respect to what you need to do 

with the message,after having encrypted it, in fact, using 

the same time values, it can be decrypted. 

Tip: the program does not recognize spaces, so for better 

understanding we recommend the use of special signs such as "-"
